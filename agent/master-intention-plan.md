# Master Intention Plan

Status: active

## Intent

Lost Pages should feel like a clean comic-book companion, not a dense PDF viewer or overdecorated single-page mockup.

The launcher should help a phone user quickly understand the project, pick a page, scan or open the AR route, and continue into the book view only when they want the full composition.

## Product Direction

- Keep the eight-page AR magazine structure.
- Present pages as simple comic-book spreads.
- Keep QR and AR actions obvious.
- Keep visual detail in service of readability.
- Use motion as light feedback, not spectacle.
- Keep the 3D book view available as a secondary view.

## Visual Direction

- Clean comic panels.
- Two-page spread rhythm on desktop.
- Single readable cards on phone.
- Warm paper, strong ink, subtle glow.
- Avoid a heavy sepia wash in the overall book/launcher tone.
- Light reactive animation from pointer movement and scroll visibility.

## Agent Rule

Future visual changes should update this file only when the master direction changes. Small implementation notes belong in `agent/cleanup-plan.md` or `agent/run-log.md`.
